APP NAME
UnicornApps: AI Descriptions

SHORT DESCRIPTION
Snap a product photo and get AI descriptions in Arabic, French and English.

FULL DESCRIPTION
UnicornApps writes your product listings for you, starting from a single photo.

Take a picture of any product, or choose one from your gallery. The app looks at the image and writes a full listing: a product title, a description, short selling points, and captions for social media. You get all of it from the same photo, in three languages, Arabic, French, and English.

It was built for the people who list products every day. Online shop owners, dropshippers, and small marketing teams selling on Noon, Amazon, Shopify, Instagram, and local marketplaces. Writing fresh copy for every new product is slow, and most writing tools only do a good job in English. UnicornApps is made to work properly in the languages sellers in this region actually use.

The app is straightforward. Open it, point your camera at a product, and wait a few seconds. You get a finished listing you can read, edit, and paste into your store. If a result is not quite right, refine it until the wording sounds like your shop. Everything you generate is kept in your history, so you can find it again later.

A few things you can do with UnicornApps:
Describe a product without facing an empty text box.
Sell in Arabic, French, and English without rewriting everything by hand.
Get a title, a description, selling points, and social captions in one step.
Keep your past work in one place instead of scattered notes and screenshots.

UnicornApps is free to use on Android. You sign in with your email and you can start generating straight away.

The app is made by an independent developer in Morocco, for sellers across the Arabic and French speaking world who deserve good tools in their own language.

About your data: you sign in with your email address. The photos you submit are sent to an AI service to generate your content, and the listings you create are saved to your account so you can find them later. You can delete your account and everything in it at any time from the Account page inside the app.
